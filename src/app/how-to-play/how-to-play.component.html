01

CHOOSE A CATEGORY

First, choose a word category,
like animals or movies. The
computer then randomly selects
a secret word from that topic
and shows you blanks for each
letter of the word

02

GUESS LETTERS

Take turns guessing letters. The
computer fills in the relevant
blank spaces if your guess is
correct. If it's wrong, you lose
some health, which empties
after eight incorrect guesses.

03

WIN OR LOSE

You win by guessing all the
letters in the word before your
health runs out. If the health
bar empties before you guess
the word, you lose
